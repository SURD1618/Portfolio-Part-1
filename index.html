About me

Hello! I'm Rohan Das, a recent graduate with a Master's degree in Computer Science and Applications from Christ Deemed to be University, Bangalore. My journey in the tech world has been deeply rooted in both theoretical and practical domains, with extensive experience in software development. I am proficient in Java, React, and web development, and I have a passion for building innovative applications that solve real-world problems.

During my undergraduate studies, I worked on various projects ranging from Java-based web applications to weather data integrations, a blockchain project to secure documents and even a Chrome extension focused on email automation. Currently, I am further enhancing my skills by working on full-stack projects and exploring new technologies such as AI and cloud computing.

I am constantly seeking opportunities to learn and grow, with a focus on making meaningful contributions to the tech industry.
